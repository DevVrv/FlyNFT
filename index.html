Project itself

FLYNFT is a premium co-ivestmetn project. High cost premium actives like planes and yachts are unreachable by regular investors and will not gain any profit if you just simply buy them. This type of premium assets are more about status, constant depreciation and lots of other risks and costs for rich owners. But if you are able to provide wise service, exploitation and utilisation for yacht or business jet's fleet - you will get high profitable business. This is exactly what successful operating companies do.

FLYNFT brings together WEB3-investment tools, high class servise companies and premium assets to let everyone reach this hight. Our business analytics and operating-experts team wisely choose premium assets for funding and service companies to partner with. We "cut" premium asset on NFT-pieces and share operating companies revenues with our investors.
